Message From Our Principal

Welcome To Cisira Combined Primary

"It is with great pride and excitement that I welcome you to our school. At Cisira Primary, we are a community dedicated to fostering a love for learning and personal growth."

We believe in a holistic approach to education, one that balances academic rigor with artistic expression, physical activity, and character development. Our talented and dedicated staff works tirelessly to create a safe and inspiring environment for every child.

Our mission extends beyond textbooks and classrooms. We strive to cultivate compassionate, responsible citizens who will contribute positively to our society. With a rich history dating back to 1995, we continue to evolve while maintaining our core values of excellence, integrity, and community.
